<p><ul><li>Omni Directional</li><li>Rotating Directional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Outdoor Emergency Alert System market is categorized into two primary types: Omni-Directional and Rotating Directional systems. Omni-Directional systems emit alerts in all directions, ensuring widespread coverage and quick dissemination of information to a broad audience. In contrast, Rotating Directional systems focus on specific areas, directing sound to targeted locations while minimizing noise pollution in unintended zones. Both types are essential for effective emergency communication, catering to different needs based on geographic and urban factors.</p></p>
