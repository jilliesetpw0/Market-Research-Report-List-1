<p><p>The Aircraft Navigation Lights market encompasses three primary types of lighting: LED lamps, halogen lamps, and xenon lamps. LED lamps are favored for their energy efficiency, longevity, and low maintenance needs, making them a popular choice for modern aircraft. Halogen lamps offer brighter illumination and quicker response times, although their lifespan is shorter. Xenon lamps provide intense brightness and are often used in high-performance applications, but can be less energy-efficient and have a higher operational cost compared to LEDs.</p></p>
