<p><ul><li>PTH Type</li><li>Buildup IVH Type</li><li>Hybrid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Probe Card PCB market features three main types: PTH Type, Buildup IVH Type, and Hybrid Type. PTH (Plated Through Hole) Type utilizes through-holes for connections, offering durability for high-density applications. Buildup IVH (Interconnect Via Hole) Type features multilayer structures that enhance signal integrity, ideal for advanced packaging technologies. Hybrid Type combines materials and technologies from both PTH and Buildup IVH, providing versatility and performance benefits for various semiconductor testing needs, catering to evolving industry demands.</p></p>
