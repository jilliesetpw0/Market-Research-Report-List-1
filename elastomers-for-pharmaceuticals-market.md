<p><strong>The Elastomers for Pharmaceuticals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoset Elastomers</li><li>Thermoplastic Elastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elastomers for pharmaceuticals market is segmented into thermoset elastomers and thermoplastic elastomers. Thermoset elastomers, once cured, maintain their shape and structure, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and chemical resistance, such as seals and gaskets in drug packaging. In contrast, thermoplastic elastomers can be remolded and processed like plastics, offering flexibility and ease of manufacturing. They are often used for components requiring dynamic performance, like stoppers and tubing in pharmaceutical delivery systems.</p></p>
