<p><p>The Perfluorosulfonic Acid (PFSA) membrane market can be categorized into two main types: extruded membranes and dispersed membranes. Extruded membranes are produced through a continuous extrusion process, resulting in uniform thickness and properties, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Dispersed membranes, on the other hand, are created by dispersing PFSA materials in a solvent, allowing for versatility in form and application. Both types play crucial roles in fuel cells and various electrochemical processes, impacting energy efficiency and performance.</p></p>
